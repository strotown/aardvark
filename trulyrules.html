All entries will be judged equally on the quality and uniqueness of their entry.

Judging the quality and creativity of a photo is subjective, but there are some general criteria that can be used to evaluate a photograph:

  1. Answer to the Question: The uniqueness, relevance and creativity used to answer the question.
  2. Use of the Hashtag: Inclusion of the Promotional Hashtag #TRUSUMMER (Required).
  3. Composition: The way the elements in the photo are arranged and how they interact with each other can greatly affect the overall impact of the photo. A well-composed photo will have a pleasing balance and harmony between the different elements.
  4. Exposure: Exposure refers to how bright or dark a photo is. A good photo should have the right exposure for the subject matter, with the right balance of light and shadow.
  5. Focus: The focus of a photo can draw the viewer's attention to a particular subject or detail. A well-focused photo will have the subject in sharp focus, while the background is blurred.
  6. Color: The use of color in a photo can greatly affect the mood and tone of the image. A good photo will have colors that are balanced and harmonious.
  7. Creativity: A creative photo will have a unique and interesting perspective, composition, or subject matter that sets it apart from other photos.
  8. Storytelling: A photo that tells a story or evokes an emotion can be particularly impactful. A good photo should be able to convey a message or tell a story without the need for words.

These are just a few criteria, and there are many other factors that can affect the quality and creativity of a photo. Ultimately, what makes a photo "good" is subjective and depends on the judge’s own unique taste and preferences. The winning entry will be solely based upon these distinct tastes & preferences and will not be subject to any outside opinions of definitions of the above description.

The following guidelines should be followed when submitting a photo:

  • Not depict the act of drinking
  • Not portray, encourage, or condone drunk-driving
  • Not depict situations where alcoholic beverages are being consumed excessively, in an irresponsible way, or in any way illegally.
  • Not portray persons in a state of intoxication or in any way suggest that intoxication is acceptable conduct.
  • Not portray or imply illegal activity of any kind.
  • Not portray sexual passion, promiscuity, or any other amorous activity.
  • Not employ any symbol, gesture, or cartoon character that is intended to appeal primarily to persons below the legal purchase age.
  • Not employ any entertainment figure or group that is intended to appeal primarily to persons below the legal purchase age.
  • Not depict Santa Claus.
  • Not associate or portray drinking alcoholic beverages before or during activities which require a high degree of alertness or coordination.
  • Not contain any lewd or indecent language or images.
  • Not employ religion or religious themes.
  • Not disparage competing alcoholic beverages.
  • Depict retail outlets as well kept and respectable establishments.
  • Not show littering or otherwise improper disposal of containers, unless the scenes are used clearly to promote anti-littering and/or recycling.
  • Not show any trademarks or copyrights other than the Sponsor’s trademarks or copyrights.
